The upside

What decentralization actually buys you.

No middlemen

Premiums flow to the pools that actually carry your risk — not to branch offices, brokers and ad budgets.

Instant settlement

Verified event → automatic payout. The contract can't slow-walk you, lose your file, or 'escalate to a supervisor.'

Transparent pools

Every pool's capital, every policy, every payout is on-chain. Solvency isn't a rating-agency opinion — it's a public number.

Global by default

2 billion people are underinsured because branches don't reach them. A wallet does.

Who benefits

Built for both sides of the risk.

Policyholders get protection. Underwriters get yield. The protocol matches them without taking a house cut for shareholders.
